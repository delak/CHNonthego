Take urgent action

(first trimester)
  1. Give TT injection, if indicated
  2. If available, provide iron/folate supplements: 1 iron tablet (60 mg) + 1 folic acid tablet (0.5 mg) to be taken daily
  3. If available, provide multivitamin supplements
  4. Counsel client on:

    1. Care during pregnancy

    2. Danger signs

    3. Birth preparedness and complication readiness

    4. Substance abuse

    5. STI prevention and hygiene

    6. Nutrition

    7. Start FP counselling

    8. When to return to a health center, hospital or CHPS facility

  5. Refer client to receive focused ANC from a midwife
